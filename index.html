Things to Know Before Workout

Active Recovery

This is one way to spend your “rest” day. So instead of lounging on the couch all day you’ll schedule some sort of low-intensity activity like light walking or gentle yoga. when you work out, you’re breaking down muscle fibers, and recovery is when the real magic happens as your muscles rebuild stronger.

Aerobic Exercise

Often we call all cardio ‘aerobics,’ but aerobic is actually a specific energy system, “[Energy systems] relate to how your body produces energy to fuel your workouts.” During aerobic exercise, your body uses oxygen for energy, which helps keep you moving for an extended period of time.
